Attefall temple
2020

A small temple I designed to fit into the limitations of a swedish "Attefallshus" that can be built without a building permit. Those limits were 25 square meters in area and 4 meters in height when this was designed, but the permitted area was recently increased to 30 square meters. It is an unbuilt project, as it would cost approximately 104 million SEK for the materials alone.
